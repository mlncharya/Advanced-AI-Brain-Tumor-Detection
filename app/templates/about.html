About project

With the growing number of neurological disorders, it is crucial to detect brain tumors early and accurately for effective treatment. In this study, we introduce a new method that combines the strengths of Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Long Short-Term Memory (LSTM) networks to improve the accuracy of brain tumor detection in medical images. CNNs are used to extract important features from the complex patterns in medical images, while LSTMs are applied to understand sequential and time-related patterns in the data. By integrating these two approaches, our model can learn both spatial and temporal features effectively, making it better at identifying subtle and dynamic changes linked to brain tumors.
